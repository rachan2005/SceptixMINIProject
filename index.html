Free and Open Source Software

Welcome to the World of FOSS

Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) is a movement that promotes the use and distribution of software that can be freely used, modified, and shared by anyone. FOSS has been growing in popularity in recent years, and has become an important part of the technology landscape.


On this website, we aim to provide you with resources and information about FOSS, including how to get started with FOSS software, the benefits of using FOSS, and how to get involved in the FOSS community.
